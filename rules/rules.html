RULES

If a player or the dealer is dealt an Ace and a 10-value card on deal = INSTANT BLACKJACK!!

  • Place bets before the cards are dealt.
  • Player is dealt two cards. Dealer gets one face up, one face down.
  • Players can hit (get another card) or stand (keep current cards).
  • After players, the dealer shows their hidden card and must hit until they have at least 17.
  • Beat the dealer without going over 21 to win.
  • A Stand-off means your bet is returned.
