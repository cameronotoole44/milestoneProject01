RULES

If a player or the dealer is dealt an Ace and a 10-value card = BLACKJACK!!

  • Players get two cards; the dealer gets one up, one down.
  • Players can hit (get another card) or stand (keep current cards).
  • After players, the dealer shows their hidden card and must hit until they have at least 16.
  • If you go over 21, you lose. Closest to 21 wins.
  • Beat the dealer without busting to win your bet; otherwise, you lose it.
  • A tie means your bet is returned.
